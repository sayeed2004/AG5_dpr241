Things About Toyota

What is Toyota

"Toyota Motor Corporation was founded by the Toyoda family. The word "Toyoda" uses ten Japanese strokes to write while "Toyota" uses only eight. Eight is considered a lucky number in Japanese culture. For these reasons "Toyota" was chosen as the company's name."

Different Kinds of Car in Toyota

There are many kinds of cars that Toyota has. For instance, Camry,Corolla, Rav4, Avalon, and more.

Who is the founder of Toyota?

"The company was founded by Kiichiro Toyoda in 1937, as a spinoff from his father's company Toyota Industries to create automobiles. Three years earlier, in 1934, while still a department of Toyota Industries, it created its first product, the Type A engine, and its first passenger car in 1936, the Toyota AA."
